Michaela's Journey

Hello, my name is Michaela.
I'm a 23 year old with a past that, personally, I believe isn't so bright.
October 4th, 2022 is when I finally understood how to live my life.

This is me on April 14, 2018.
It was one of the happiest times that I could remember myself up until now.
I was rebuilt, renewed, rediscovered.
But I let all of that go, packed happiness up in a box, and forgot where I put it.


I realize that this may seem a little sad, but I'm actually here to tell you
how I found it again. It wasn't easy, and to be honest, I'm not fully there myself,
but maybe this will help someone else out along the way.


On October 4th I had an epiphany. It's one that shouldn't have taken me this long to figure out:
We only get one life to live, and we get to choose how we live it.
So do I really want to live the rest of my life miserable and unhappy?
Do I want to leave this Earth knowing that I wasted the only life I get?

Yes

NO!
